FAQ

What is KhmerX?

KhmerX is a Cambodia-focused micro‑lending information platform using Telegram Mini App and ABA transfers.

How does borrowing work?

Open Mini App → bind ABA → choose amount/term → review Receive/Repay → publish request → complete ABA transfer with proof records.

Does KhmerX support ABA?

Yes. Transfers are completed via ABA. KhmerX does not hold user funds.

Does KhmerX support Telegram?

Yes. Telegram Mini App is the primary entry. Telegram Bot supports notifications and help.

Do I need an ID card?

It depends on verification requirements inside the Mini App. KhmerX does not guarantee approval.

How do I repay?

Repay via ABA by the agreed due time and keep proof (screenshots / transaction IDs).

What happens if overdue?

Overdue may affect credit/limits and can cause disputes. Contact Telegram if you hit issues.
