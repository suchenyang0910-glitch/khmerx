FAQ

What is KhmerX?

KhmerX is a Cambodia-focused micro lending information platform that connects local users via Telegram Mini App and ABA transfer.

How does borrowing work?

Open Mini App → bind ABA → choose amount/term → publish request → complete ABA transfer and repayment proof records.

Does KhmerX support ABA?

Yes. Transfers are completed via ABA. KhmerX does not hold user funds.

What happens if overdue?

Overdue may reduce credit level and borrowing limit, and may trigger dispute and collection workflow.
