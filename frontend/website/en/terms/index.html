KhmerX Rules & Agreements

Terms of Service

Before using KhmerX, please confirm and understand the relevant rules.

KhmerX Service Statement

KhmerX is a micro-lending information matching platform for users in Cambodia.

  • KhmerX is NOT a bank
  • KhmerX does NOT accept deposits
  • KhmerX does NOT guarantee successful loans
  • KhmerX does NOT guarantee returns

User Eligibility

  • Use Telegram for registration and operations
  • Provide genuine and valid ABA account information
  • Provide genuine identity information
  • Comply with local laws and regulations

KhmerX reserves the right to restrict or refuse high-risk accounts.

Borrowing Rules

Borrowing Details
Before borrowing, you must confirm the loan amount, received amount, repayment amount, and loan term.

Fees
Loans may include fixed fees and service charges.

User Confirmation
Submitting a loan request constitutes confirmation of all related fees and rules.

Repayment Rules

Repayment Method
Users must repay via an ABA transfer.

Receipt Upload
After transferring, users must upload a screenshot of the repayment receipt.

Due Date Reminder
The system may send a reminder 24 hours before the due date.

Risks & Platform Disclaimer

KhmerX is merely an information platform and bears no responsibility for transaction risks.

  • The platform does not guarantee loans will be funded or repaid on time.
  • The platform is not liable for loan losses, transaction losses, or user disputes.
  • Users must verify the authenticity and risk of counterparties themselves.
  • Any transaction may involve third-party risks which the platform does not guarantee.

Violations

  • Providing false information
  • Using multiple accounts to manipulate credit or limits
  • Forging transaction or repayment receipts
  • Maliciously defaulting or refusing to repay
  • Any other fraudulent behavior

KhmerX reserves the right to permanently restrict violating accounts.

Platform Restrictions & Security

To protect the platform and user security, KhmerX reserves the right to:

  • Restrict high-risk accounts
  • Freeze abnormal transaction behavior
  • Dynamically adjust or limit loan amounts
  • Refuse service to specific users
