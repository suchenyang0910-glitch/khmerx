How to Borrow with KhmerX

KhmerX helps Cambodia users publish small short-term borrowing requests via Telegram Mini App and complete transactions through ABA transfer.

Step 1
Open Telegram Mini App
Step 2
Bind your ABA account and phone number
Step 3
Choose amount and term
Step 4
Review received amount and repayment amount
Step 5
Submit request and complete ABA transfer + repayment records

Borrowing limits

  • • Amount/term can depend on your history and risk signals
  • • KhmerX does not guarantee approval, payout, or any outcome
  • • Always verify Receive/Repay before completing

Repayment rules

  • • Repay through ABA by the agreed due time
  • • Keep proof screenshots/transaction IDs
  • • Contact Telegram support if you hit issues

Risk notice

  • • KhmerX is an information service, not a bank or guarantor
  • • Overdue can affect your credit/limits and cause disputes
  • • Users should assess risk and keep their own proofs
