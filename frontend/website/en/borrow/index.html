FAQ

How to start borrowing?

Open the KhmerX Telegram Mini App, bind your ABA account, and you can publish a borrowing request.

Does it support ABA?

Yes. All loan disbursements and repayments must be completed via ABA bank transfers.

How fast will I receive the money?

Once matched, the lender will transfer directly to your ABA account, usually within minutes.

What happens if I am overdue?

Overdue payments will decrease your credit level, potentially blocking future requests. Lenders may also pursue debt collection.

How to increase my limit?

Maintain a good borrowing history and repay on time. The system will automatically increase your limit based on your credit performance.
