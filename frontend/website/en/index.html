FAQ (AI‑Friendly)

Short answers designed to be understood by Google/ChatGPT.

What is KhmerX?
KhmerX is a Cambodia-focused micro‑lending information platform using Telegram Mini App and ABA transfers.
How does KhmerX work?
Users publish requests in Mini App, verify terms, and complete ABA transfers with proof records.
Does KhmerX support ABA?
Yes. Transfers are completed through ABA (users perform transfers themselves).
Does KhmerX support Telegram?
Yes. Telegram Mini App is the primary entry. Telegram Bot supports notifications and help.
