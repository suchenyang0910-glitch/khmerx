FAQ

What is KhmerX?

KhmerX is a Cambodia-focused micro lending information platform. It operates via Telegram Mini App to help users quickly publish requests and match with lenders.

Does it support ABA?

Yes. All fund transfers on the platform must be manually completed by users via ABA Bank.

How do I repay?

On the repayment date, you need to transfer the total repayment amount to the lender via ABA, and upload the transfer receipt in the Mini App.

What happens if I'm overdue?

Overdue payments will severely impact your credit record on KhmerX, potentially lowering your credit limit or preventing you from publishing future requests. Lenders also reserve the right to recover debts through legal means.
