What is KhmerX?

KhmerX is a Cambodia-focused micro lending information platform. It helps local users publish small short-term borrowing requests via Telegram Mini App and complete transactions through ABA transfer.

KhmerX

KhmerX is an information and credit record tool for small local borrowing requests. KhmerX does not guarantee approval, returns, deposits, or repayment.
