FAQ

Does KhmerX require Telegram?

Yes, all KhmerX services are provided via the Telegram Mini App for security and convenience.

How do I open the Mini App?

Go to @KhmerXBot and click the Open App button at the bottom or in the Menu.

Does it support ABA?

Yes, all fund transactions are completed directly between users' ABA accounts.

Why can't I open it?

Please check your network connection or ensure your Telegram is updated to the latest version supporting WebApp features.
