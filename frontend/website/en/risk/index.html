KhmerX Safety & Rules

Risk & Safety Notice

Please confirm and understand the associated risks before using KhmerX.

KhmerX Service Description

KhmerX is a micro lending information service platform for Cambodian users. Users can publish requests via Telegram Mini App and complete transactions through ABA transfers. We provide transparent information matching.

KhmerX is NOT a bank
KhmerX does NOT accept deposits
KhmerX does NOT guarantee profits
KhmerX does NOT guarantee loans

4 Major Risks to Read Before Borrowing

Risk 1

Receive Amount

Confirm the difference between the actual receive amount and repayment amount due to upfront fees.

Risk 2

Duration

Confirm the exact duration and due date to avoid unexpected overdue status.

Risk 3

Overdue Consequence

Overdue may lead to decreased limits and restrictions on future borrowing requests.

Risk 4

Transaction Check

Carefully verify ABA transfer information and ensure the validity of receipts.

Borrowing Risks

Borrowing Fees

Loans may include fixed fees and service charges, deducted upfront at disbursement.

Confirm in Advance

Please confirm before borrowing:

  • Actual receive amount
  • Total repayment amount
  • Borrowing duration

Credit Impact

Your repayment history will directly affect your credit rating and future available limits.

Overdue & Credit Rules

Due Reminder

The system will send a reminder to your Telegram 24 hours before the due date.

Overdue Consequences

Overdue may result in:

  • Credit score drop
  • Lower borrowing limit
  • Restriction on new requests

Severe Overdue

Severe or malicious overdue may result in permanent restriction from platform services.

KhmerX Official Statement

KhmerX acts solely as an information service platform. All financial transactions occur directly between users' ABA accounts.

The platform explicitly DOES NOT guarantee:

  • Borrowing requests will be successfully matched
  • Lent funds will generate profit
  • Lent funds will be repaid on time
Users must bear and confirm all transaction risks themselves.

Safety Advice & Anti-Scam Guide

Official Telegram

Ensure you are using the official Telegram Bot and Mini App. Do not trust lookalike accounts.

Anti-Scam

KhmerX official support will NEVER ask you to transfer money to private accounts.

Keep Proofs

Retain ABA transfer records and receipt screenshots for every transaction as proof in case of disputes.

FAQ

Does KhmerX guarantee my loan will be funded?

No. KhmerX is a matching platform. Funding depends on whether a lender accepts your request.

Why is the received amount different?

Fees are deducted upfront. The actual receive amount is the borrow amount minus fees.

What happens if I'm overdue?

Your credit score will drop, your limit will decrease, and you may be banned. Lenders can also pursue debt collection.

Is KhmerX a bank?

No. KhmerX does not accept deposits or lend its own money. We only provide an information matching service.
